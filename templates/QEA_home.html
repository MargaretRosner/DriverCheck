DESCRIPTION

We chose to model the dynamics of a passive dynamic walker for our final QEA project because we wanted to get more expereince with dynamics as well as simulations and physical modeling.

Why Passive Walking?

Passive Dynamic walking is the most efficient form of walking because it propels a peidal mechanism using only gravity.
