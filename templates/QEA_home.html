ABOUT THE PROJECT

DESCRIPTION

Settling the classic argument of "who is the better driver" through analysis of driver jerk and aggressiveness.

USERS

Any individual looking to better understand driving style
We envision our app being used my a diverse audience which could include:

  • Parents of New Drivers
  • Driving Schools
  • Car Rental Companies

Our MVP

Our MVP analyzes two different aspects of driving, aggressiveness and Jerk, based on accelerometer and gyroscope data collected using a user's phone and synthesizes the analysis.
We then visualize this data in a useful and compelling manner so drivers can easily understand and compare their driving styles.

Sensor Selection

We choose to leverage the sensors already present in a users phone to create our application.
We used the PowerSense iphone app to collect the data needed to create our app.
